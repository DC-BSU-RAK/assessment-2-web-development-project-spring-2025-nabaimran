Evolution of Lara Croft

1996

Original Lara (Tomb Raider)

The first-ever appearance of Lara Croft. Polygonal, bold, and groundbreaking for the time. The original Tomb Raider set the standard for 3D action-adventure gaming.

1997

Tomb Raider II

Lara returns with improved graphics, new weapons, and vehicles. Her iconic dual pistols and braid became gaming legend.

1999

Tomb Raider: The Last Revelation

Introduced flashbacks to Lara’s youth and featured a more refined model and deeper narrative focus.

2003

The Angel of Darkness

A darker, grittier Lara with a new stealth approach. Although the game was controversial, it marked a shift toward a more emotional, serious tone.

2006

Legend, Anniversary, Underworld

Crystal Dynamics rebooted the franchise. Lara got a facelift and better physics, motion capture, and platforming mechanics.

2013

Tomb Raider Reboot

A gritty origin story. This Lara is young, vulnerable, but fiercely determined. The reboot humanized her like never before.

2015

Rise of the Tomb Raider

Polished gameplay and an emotionally driven story. Lara explores Siberia and fights a mysterious organization.

2018

Shadow of the Tomb Raider

The climax of the reboot trilogy. Lara becomes the Tomb Raider, confronting her past and embracing her destiny.

2023

Upcoming Tomb Raider (Rumored)

Crystal Dynamics is working on the next-generation Tomb Raider powered by Unreal Engine 5. Fans eagerly await Lara’s next chapter.
