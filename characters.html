Main Characters

Lara Croft

The fearless archaeologist and adventurer at the center of the Tomb Raider series. Intelligent, resourceful, and relentless.

Jonah Maiava

Lara’s loyal companion and close friend, known for his strength, compassion, and support throughout her quests.

Jacob

A mysterious immortal and leader of the Remnant, Jacob plays a key role in guiding Lara through her spiritual and historical discoveries.

Ana

A complex antagonist who manipulates events for Trinity. Her connection to Lara’s past adds emotional depth to the conflict.

Lord Richard Croft

Lara's father, a noble archaeologist whose mysterious disappearance motivates Lara’s quest for truth.

Sam Nishimura

Lara's best friend and documentary filmmaker, who often finds herself caught in the chaos of Lara's adventures.<

Zip

A tech-savvy ally who provides Lara with support and intel during missions.

Winston Smith

Lara's loyal and humorous butler, featured prominently in the earlier games.

Alister Fletcher

A historian and researcher who often helps Lara in her investigations.

Reyes

A tough mechanic and crew member from the 2013 reboot, protective and headstrong.

Enemies

Mathias Vogel

A ruthless antagonist from the 2013 reboot, seeking the power of the lost queen Himiko on Yamatai island.

Jacqueline Natla

One of the original villains from the early Tomb Raider games — cunning, immortal, and power-hungry.

Trinity Organization

An ancient and dangerous organization seeking mystical artifacts for global domination. Lara constantly opposes their plans.

Amanda Evert

Lara’s former friend turned enemy, corrupted by an ancient power.

Kostantin

High-ranking Trinity operative, ruthless enforcer, and Ana’s brother.
